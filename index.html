Screen Printing

is a printing technique where a fine mesh is used to transfer ink onto a substrate, except in areas made impermeable to the ink by a blocking stencil. Your chosen Jute, Juco, Canvas or Leather product can be customized using the screen printing method in single or multicolor artworks as required by you. "Vibrant screen-printed jute, cotton, and leather products, alongside exquisite handicrafts, beautifully blend style and sustainability for eco-conscious individuals seeking unique and environmentally friendly accessories." "Elevate your style with our screen-printed collection of eco-friendly jute bags, cotton bags, leather goods, and exquisite handicrafts. Combining artistry, sustainability, and functionality for a truly unique and conscious fashion statement."

Embroidery

is the craft of decorating the fabric or other material (including leather) by using a needle to apply thread or yarn. Your chosen Jute, Juco, Canvas or Leather product can be custom embroidered in single or multicolor designs as required by you. "Discover the intricate art of embroidery on our range of jute bags, cotton bags, leather goods, and handicrafts. Each piece showcases the perfect blend of craftsmanship and creativity, adding a touch of elegance to your style."

Digital Printing

is the process of printing digital images onto fabric or leather. Digital files such as .pdf or any desktop publishing files can be sent directly to the digital printing machine to print with no limitation on the number of colors. Your chosen Jute, Juco, Canvas or Leather product can be digitally printed in your required artwork. "Our skilled artisans meticulously stitch intricate designs onto jute bags, cotton bags, leather goods, and handicrafts, combining traditional techniques with artistic flair. Each piece undergoes a meticulous embroidery process, resulting in stunning and unique creations."

Blind Embossing

is a marking technique that does not use ink, which is why it is also called embossed or hot stamping. Blind embossing creates a relief on leather, which gives the item an awesome look. "With blind embossing, our expert craftsmen create tactile impressions on jute bags, cotton bags, leather goods, and handicrafts. The subtle yet elegant raised designs add a touch of sophistication and texture to each piece, showcasing the artistry of our artisans."

Foil Transfer

When any colored foil (or metallic foil) is used at time of Blind embossing process. The color from the foil gets transferred on leather which gives the item an elegant look. Your chosen Leather product can be customized with your logo using the foil transfer method. Your selected Leather product can be blind embossed with your logo. "Using foil transfer, we apply lustrous metallic accents to jute bags, cotton bags, leather goods, and handicrafts. Through heat and pressure, the foil adheres to the surface, creating a dazzling and eye-catching embellishment that adds a touch of luxury to each piece."

Laser Cutting

is a technology that uses a laser light beam to cut materials (like leather). Laser cutting works by directing the output of a high-power laser through optical lenses and mirrors. Your chosen Leather product can be custom laser cut in designs and patterns as required by you. "Our laser cutting technique precisely etches intricate patterns onto leather products and handicrafts. The high-powered laser beam effortlessly cuts through the material, resulting in precise and detailed designs that elevate the craftsmanship and aesthetics of each piece."

Laser Engraving

is the method of using lasers machines to engrave leather or any object. Laser Engraving leave a marks on an object which includes color change due to charring. The technique does not involve the use of any kind of ink. "Laser engraving breathes life into leather products and handicrafts. Our powerful lasers etch designs onto the surface, creating elegant and lasting impressions. The result is a seamless blend of artistry and functionality, showcasing the beauty of leather." Your chosen Leather product can be custom engraved as required by you.

Quilting

is the term given to the process of joining a minimum of three layers of fabric together An array of stitches is passed through all layers of the fabric to create a three dimensional padded surface. The three layers are typically referred to as the top layer, with sponge in between and the back lining fabric. Your chosen Canvas or Leather product can be custom quilted in designs or patterns as required by you. "Our quilting process involves meticulously stitching layers of leather, creating stunning textures on products and handicrafts. The precise stitching patterns enhance durability and aesthetic appeal, showcasing the perfect harmony of craftsmanship and style."

Custom Labeling

No product is fully complete, without labels in your brand. We also offer a wide variety of labels. You can choose to have a printed or woven Product Label or a simple Care Label perhaps an Embossed Leather Label with your brand. Alternatively you can choose to have a Barcode or QR Label with your brand "With custom labeling, we personalize jute bags, cotton bags, leather products, and handicrafts. Your brand or design is expertly printed or embossed onto a label, adding a distinctive touch of identity and quality to each item."

Custom Packing

Each product requires a different way of packing. Products made in your brand can be custom packed as required by you. We offer 100% customized packing options for :- Jute and Jute Cotton Products Cotton and Canvas Bags Leather Wallets, Purses and Bags
